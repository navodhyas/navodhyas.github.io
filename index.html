Navodhya Samarakoon

PhD Candidate in Economics  ·  University of Michigan, Ann Arbor

Welcome to my site! I am a third-year PhD student in the Department of Economics at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. Before coming to Michigan, I worked in D.C. as as Junior Stanley S. Surrey Fellow at the Office of Tax Analysis in the U.S. Department of Treasury.

My research lies in the areas of spatial economics, trade, public finance, and labor economics. I focus on the taxation of multinational companies, place-based policies, and the spatial inequality in the United States. At this stage of my career, I am keen to engage in a broad range of public policy topics.
